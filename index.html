Short Bio

Dr. Sean Bin Yang received his Ph.D degree in the Department of Computer Science at the Aalborg University, Aalborg, Denmark, in 2022. My supervisors are Prof. Bin Yang and Assoc. Prof. Jilin Hu. I am also a faculty member in the Center for Data-Intensive Systems (Daisy) with Prof. Christian S. Jensen.

My research interests include:

  • Representation Learning: Un/self-supervised Representation Learning
  • Data Mining: Spatial-temporal Data Mining
  • Reinforcement Learning: Autoscaling in the cloud
  • Graph Neural Network: Traffic Cost Forecasting, Time Series Forcasting
  • Computer Vision: Detection
  • Control Theory Applications: Traffic Flow Control
